Dirty Bomb is an original IP from Splash Damage Studios, the creators of Enemy Territory.
Dirty Bomb features modern class-based gameplay on progressive mixed-objective maps. 23 playable 'mercs' filling five roles: medic, engineer, assault, fire support, and recon.
No longer actively developed, the game is now free on steam and has constantly full 7v7 pubs
