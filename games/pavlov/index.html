Pavlov VR is the first release from vankrupt studios and is available on SteamVR. There is a new port for Quest headsets called Pavlov Shack which should have many of the same features. Originally a Counter-Strike clone in VR, the ease of mapping and modding has lead to many custom maps and modes being created.
