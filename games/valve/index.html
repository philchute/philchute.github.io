Valve corporation was founded in 1996 by Gabe Newell and Mike Harrington. PC gaming is defined by their landmark 1998 title Half-life and the mods that surrounded it. Valve's follow-up titles rank among the best games of all time.

All of Valve's early titles are available in a package on Steam: Valve Complete Pack on Steam
This package sells for $65 but goes on sale for only $13: Valve Complete Pack on SteamDB
